Smart mode

Reads each page and tunes contrast and brightness for the most natural dark theme — automatically, no fiddling.

02

Saved presets

Reading, Night Owl, or your own. Dial brightness, contrast and sepia once, then reuse the look anywhere.

03

Per-site control

Love a site's own dark theme? Switch Dark Mode Lite off for it with one tap — and it stays that way.

04

Every change lands the moment you make it. No refresh, no flash of white, no waiting.
