# Privacy Policy — Dark Mode - Lite

Dark Mode - Lite does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any user data.

- **No data collection.** The extension has no analytics, no accounts, and makes
  no network requests to us or to any third party.
- **Local settings only.** Your preferences (theme mode, brightness/contrast/sepia,
  custom presets, and per-site on/off choices) are saved with Chrome's
  `storage.sync` API. They remain in your browser profile and, if you're signed
  in to Chrome, are synced by Google across your devices. We never see them.
- **No page data access.** The content script only applies styling to the pages
  you visit. It does not read, record, or transmit page content, form input, or
  browsing history.

If this policy ever changes, the update will be published at this URL and
reflected in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.
